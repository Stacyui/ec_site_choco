When will I get my order?

▼

Answer

We aim to ensure that you receive your order as quickly as possible. Our normal delivery time is within 5-7 business days, once we ship the products from the warehouse.

How do I return or exchange an item?

▼

Answer

If you decide your Nike shoes, clothing, or gear just aren't right, you can return or exchange the item for another size or color within 30 days of receiving them,* as long as the items are unused. Follow Our Return Instructions and be sure to complete the second section of the Return and Product Size/Color Exchange Form included with your order.

How do I cancel an order?

▼

Answer

You can cancel items or orders that haven't entered the shipping process yet.

To cancell orders, go to Order History and select Cancel Orders.

How do I use a voucher?

▼

Answer

Follow these steps or watch the animated gif above to learn how to apply a code to your order:

1: Once you’re ready to buy, head over to your Shopping Cart.

2: Click the ‘Enter A Coupon’ link and enter the code in the empty field.

3: Click ‘Apply’.

4: From there you can complete your payment details and check out via one of our accepted payment options.
